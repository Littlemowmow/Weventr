# Landing Page Content & Messaging Audit — Read Everything, Change Nothing

Do NOT make any changes. I need a complete inventory of every piece of copy and messaging on this page so I can rewrite it using market research.

## What I Need

### 1. Page Sections in Scroll Order
For every section on the page, top to bottom, give me:
- The exact headline text (word for word)
- The exact subheadline or body copy (word for word)
- The exact text on any buttons or CTAs
- The exact text on any cards, badges, labels, or tags
- Any placeholder text, tooltip text, or microcopy
- What the section is trying to communicate (its purpose in the narrative)

### 2. Full Copy Inventory
List ALL written content on the page:
- Navigation labels
- Hero headline + subheadline
- Every section heading and body paragraph
- Every card title, description, and label
- Every button label and CTA
- Form field labels and placeholder text
- FAQ questions and answers (full text)
- Footer copy, taglines, and links
- Any testimonials or quotes (and note if they're real or made up)
- Any social proof numbers or claims (and whether they're real)
- Meta title and meta description

### 3. Messaging Analysis
For each section, tell me:
- What emotion or reaction is this section trying to trigger?
- What problem or desire is it addressing?
- Who is it speaking to? (the planner friend, the whole group, a solo traveler, etc.)
- Does the tone feel corporate, casual, Gen Z, generic, etc.?
- Does anything feel like placeholder copy that was never finalized?

### 4. Visual Storytelling
- What images or illustrations are shown and what story do they tell?
- What destinations or cities are featured?
- Are there any app mockups or screenshots? What do they show?
- What's the overall visual vibe? (dark/light, adventurous/calm, playful/professional)

### 5. Narrative Arc
- What story does the page tell from top to bottom?
- Is there a clear problem → solution → proof → action flow?
- Where does the narrative feel strong vs where does it lose momentum or get vague?
- What's missing that a visitor would want to know before signing up?

## Output Format

Give me this as a structured document with the exact text quoted. I need to be able to read your report and know exactly what's on the page without looking at it myself. Be specific — quote every word, don't paraphrase.
